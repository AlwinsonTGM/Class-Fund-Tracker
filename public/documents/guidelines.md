# Class Transparency & Fund Guidelines 📊

Welcome to our class fund transparency guide. This document details the policies regarding contributions and expenditures.

> [!info] Purpose of Class Fund
> The class fund is maintained to cover mutual academic needs, printing costs, group study materials, and occasional class events. All collections are logged publicly on our [[Home|Dashboard]].

## Contribution Rules 💰
1. Every student is requested to contribute **₱5.00** per active school week.
2. Payments can be settled directly with any of our whitelisted [[Portal|Class Officers]].
3. Late payments will carry over to the subsequent week without penalties.

> [!warning] Transparency Notice
> All expenses must be supported by receipt images. Officers are required to upload details immediately upon spending. You can monitor expenses in the [[Home|Recent Activity]] feed.
